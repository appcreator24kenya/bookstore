Accepted Payment Methods - Kenya Book Store

Payment methods accepted at Kenya Book Store include Mpesa, PayPal, and Card Payments. Unfortunately, we do not accept any other payment methods like direct bank deposit, Skrill, Direct mpesa payment e.t.c

Mpesa Paybill
You need to have a Mpesa account and Money in your Mpesa account. You will use our Paybill number and account number available at checkout (account number is unique to each order).
PayPal
If you have a PayPal account. You can make a payment with PayPal. PayPal payment attracts a transaction fee. The total price (Including transaction fees) will be indicated on the PayPal payment page before your Pay.
Credit/Debit Card >Accepted Payment Methods
If you do not have a PayPal account, then you can use the PayPal payment option but pay with your Visa, MasterCard, Discover, or American Express credit or debit card.
You will be required to enter your card number, Expiry date, and Security code to complete your payments. Card payments will be processed by PayPal which is a reputable online payment processor.
We will not have access to your credit card details after payment.
Transaction Expenses
You will not incur any other expense on our website before or after payment other than the price indicated on the product page.
